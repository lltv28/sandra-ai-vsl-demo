Common questions, answered

Is this a treatment program?
No. This demo is a guided reflection experience. It helps someone notice patterns and choose a next step, but it does not replace medical care, therapy, or emergency support.
What happens after I unlock it?
The app opens a simple starter plan with one task first. The first task is a two-minute craving window reflection.
Do I need to share everything at once?
No. The flow asks small questions one at a time so the screen stays calm and easy to follow.
Is this just Sandra AI doing everything automatically?
The recording shows Sandra AI as the guide. It is designed to feel like a real product flow with clear loading, checkout, and a first task after payment.
